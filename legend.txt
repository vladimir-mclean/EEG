Aim:
Show that EEG signals may be used in the task of recognition human attention and intention. Improve the classification performance of the EEG signals.


MI_LSTM_test
Classifying the EEG to three classes: imagery movement of left hand, imagery movement of right hand, rest state. Long short-term memory classification.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motor_imagery
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_short-term_memory

ERP_SVM_test
Detecting the attention to one-of-the-many visual flashing stimuli. Support Vector Machines classification.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Event-related_potential
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Support_vector_machine

ERP_AE_test
Detecting the attention to one-of-the-many visual flashing stimuli. Autoencoders classification.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Event-related_potential
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Autoencoder
